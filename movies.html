The best 3 movies i have ever seen

The godfather

The Godfather is a famous crime novel by Mario Puzo, first published in 1969, which was later adapted into a groundbreaking film series directed by Francis Ford Coppola. It focuses on the powerful Corleone family, a Mafia dynasty led by Vito Corleone, portrayed by Marlon Brando in the film. The story explores themes of loyalty, power, family, betrayal, and crime within the context of Italian-American organized crime.

Kingsman

Kingsman is a modern action-spy film franchise that combines fast-paced action, sharp wit, and a unique sense of humor, all while paying homage to classic spy films like James Bond. The series is based on the Kingsman comic book series, created by Mark Millar and Dave Gibbons.

John Wick

John Wick is an action-thriller film series created by screenwriter Derek Kolstad. The first movie, John Wick (2014), directed by Chad Stahelski, introduced audiences to the character of John Wick, portrayed by Keanu Reeves. Wick is a retired hitman who is drawn back into the violent criminal underworld after the tragic death of his wife. His wife’s passing leaves him grieving, and in one of the most heart-wrenching moments of the film, his wife’s final gift to him — a puppy named Daisy — is brutally killed by a group of Russian gangsters. This event ignites Wick’s quest for vengeance.
